What is KhetiVidhi?

KhetiVidhi is an all-in-one platform designed to revolutionize agriculture. We bridge the gap between traditional farming wisdom and modern technology, providing farmers with the tools they need to thrive in a changing world. From detecting crop diseases with your phone camera to selling produce directly to customers, we are your trusted partner every step of the way.
