KhetiVidhi

KhetiVidhi is an innovative platform designed to support farmers by providing essential information, resources, and services to improve crop health, maximize profits, and facilitate sustainable farming practices.

Learn More

In-App selling & purchasing facilities

We are providing an In-App selling and purchasing facilities to farmers & customers. This feature removes the middlemen of mandi and maximize the profit of both the parties.

Farmers can update their inventory in app by uploading the quantity of produces & customer can order the produce from their nearest available farmer.

Regular weather updates & field information

We provide regular weather updates and detailed field information, including soil type and moisture levels.

This helps farmers make informed decisions, optimizing crop management and improving agricultural productivity.

Suggestions for Pesticides, Insecticides, & Home Remedies

We offer tailored suggestions for pesticides, insecticides, and home remedies to enhance crop health and yield.

Our recommendations aim to control pests effectively while promoting sustainable and eco-friendly agricultural practices.

Disease detection in crops

We are providing a feature that will help farmers to detect the disease in the plant and give cures according to disease.

Also it will suggest home remedies (if there any) to treat disease at a lower cost.

Suggestion based on Market Demand

We are providing suggestion based on market demand

We use weather forecasts to predict when people will want to buy more of certain products. We also quickly adjust to what people are saying they want to buy. And we make sure products are available on time by following regular trade rules.
